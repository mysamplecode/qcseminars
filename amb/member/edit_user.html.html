Change Your Unique Referral Phrase

If you would like to change your Unique Referral Phrase enter a new message in the field below and if it is not already being used by another program member you can submit the form. Please be aware that any links you have already posted on a website or sent by email using your former Unique Referral Phrase will no longer work.

Your Unique Referral Phrase is used to help you refer people using something more memorable than an ID number. Pick a meaningful word or phrase that will be easily remembered and typed. For Instance, "motivate" could be chosen.

Suggestions for Unique Referral Phrases

  • Choose a single word such as "inspire" or "empower" that is easy to remember. The shorter, the better if your referral must type it out.
  • Use LOWER CASE LETTERS ONLY
  • Maximum of 20 characters including spaces.
  • Use only numbers and letters. NO SPACES or special characters--!@#$%^&*.
  • An underscore character, "_", will be used instead of a space to separate words.
