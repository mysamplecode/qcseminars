AOL Users… Check Inbox AND Spam Folder

To access the Spam Settings page from AOL WebMail

  1. Sign on to AOL WebMail by going to http://webmail.aol.com.
  2. On the upper-right, click the Settings link.
  3. In the left panel, click the Spam Settings link.
  4. The Spam Settings page will now open up.

To access the Spam Settings page from the AOL software

  1. Sign on to the AOL software.
  2. Click the Mail menu, and then click Block Unwanted Mail. Note: Alternatively, go to AOL Keyword: Spam Controls.This keyword can be accessed only within the AOL software. However, the Keyword feature is not available in AOL Desktop v10.1.
  3. The Spam Settings page will now open up.

To access the Spam Settings page from AOL Desktop v10.1

  1. Sign on to AOL Desktop v10.1.
  2. Click the Mail icon.
  3. Click the Settings menu, and then click Spam Controls.
  4. Click Edit Spam Controls.
  5. The Spam Settings page will now open up.
